4Ô∏è‚É£ Psychological Effects

1. Loneliness

Gig workers work alone, without colleagues or teams. They lack daily social interaction.

2. Powerlessness

Algorithms decide everything: pay, job availability, rankings. Workers fear "deactivation" without explanation.

3. Anxiety & Stress

  • Unpredictable income
  • Dependence on customer ratings
  • Risk of deactivation
  • No job security

4. Burnout

Constant uncertainty drains mental energy. Workers feel they must always be available.
